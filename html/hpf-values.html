🌿 Project Healing Prosperity & Freedom Values

The foundation for everything we create and every space we hold.

Project HPF (Healing, Prosperity & Freedom) is built on the belief that a better world begins with truth, love, personal sovereignty, and conscious community. Everything we create — including Our Music Fests — is rooted in these core values:

💜 Healing

  • We honor the individual and collective journey toward emotional, physical, and spiritual wholeness.
  • We believe music, art, and storytelling are powerful tools for transformation and reconnection.
  • We create safe, welcoming spaces where all people can feel seen, heard, and valued.

💰 Prosperity

  • We believe prosperity is more than material wealth — it includes peace, purpose, connection, and well-being.
  • We support systems, communities, and conversations that empower people to live free, creative, and abundant lives.
  • We elevate voices and ideas that encourage collaboration over competition.

🌾 Freedom

  • We believe every individual has the right to live in alignment with their truth, to question systems, and to choose their path.
  • We stand for freedom of thought, speech, creativity, education, and expression.
  • We oppose coercion, censorship, and the abuse of power in all forms.

🤝 Respect & Integrity

  • We treat others with respect, regardless of background, beliefs, or life choices.
  • We communicate with honesty and compassion, and hold space for differing perspectives.
  • We take full responsibility for our words, actions, and impact.

🌍 Unity in Diversity

  • We celebrate diversity of culture, sound, expression, and lived experience.
  • We believe the future is co-created — not dictated — by all people, from the ground up.
  • We aim to build bridges, not walls.

🫶 Agreement to Values

By participating in Project HPF Music Fests, each attendee, artist, speaker, vendor and contributor agrees to uphold these values in spirit and in practice, on the grounds, on stage, behind the scenes, and in all engagements related to Project HPF Music Fests.

We don't expect perfection — we honor the journey. But we do hold this as sacred space.
