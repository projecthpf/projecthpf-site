Enrich Community

Post a need. Accept a need. Pledge what you can. A rapport-driven mutual aid system where neighbors help neighbors — and every act of giving is recognized.

You don't need an account to ask for help. Members accept and pledge.

1. Post a Need

Share what you need — food, home repair, transportation, childcare, anything. Your contact stays private and is only shared with the member who accepts or pledges.

2. Community Responds

A Project HPF member can accept your whole request or pledge partial help. You'll get contact info for anyone who steps up.

3. Confirm & Earn Rapport

Once the need is met, the requester confirms it. Members earn points and tier up (Seedling → Luminary). Businesses verified here appear on Buy Local.
