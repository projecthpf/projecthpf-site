About Us

Project Healing Prosperity & Freedom is a non-profit foundation working to move humanity forward to a world full of emotionally intelligent, independent thinkers by spreading new and useful information to help people expand their scope of knowledge about life.

The goal of this foundation is to unite the hundreds of thousands of people and organizations who are working tirelessly to make this country and the world a safer and better place for every single person; no matter what their social status, skin color, beliefs, or how bad their crime.

What Do We Do?

Connect people to new and useful knowledge and products to broaden their perspective, expand their life view, promote healthier products and reform the way we do government.

We're Organizing a Revolution Like the World Has Never Seen Before!

The Mission

Project HPF believes that every single person is born with a gift and a purpose and while our individual gifts and purposes are unique, the mission is the same for all of us; to use whatever gift was given to you to move humanity forward by serving those around us with honesty, love, compassion and kindness, regardless of the consequences.

But most people never get the opportunity to serve their purpose because we are funneled into an education system that interferes with our natural ability to learn and grow. And then we are funneled into more government systems that are corrupt, profit driven and do not put the well being of humanity first.

Project HPF believes there is nothing more important to humanity than reimagining the way we do government. We must redesign our traditional systems of top down, one size fits all government to more autonomous systems of bottom up governance.

Naturally, the world, and life, are incredibly diverse, just as God or the Universe intended it to be, and there is no one right way to do life. What is right for one individual will most certainly not be right for all individuals. We must come together to celebrate our diversity, to learn and grow from each other, so that we may extinguish the powers that benefit from our division.

The Project HPF mission is to redesign our systems of top down government to be systems of bottom up governance that promote lasting peace, prosperity and freedom for all of mankind.
